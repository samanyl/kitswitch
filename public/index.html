What do we offer?

Sustainable & integrated residential conversions


90% Reduction in Design Time

With our layout optimization tool, we can fit any number of residential Kit Switch units in your space in a matter of minutes. Our tool allows any combination of 1 to 3 bedrooms and 1 to 1.5 bathrooms for each unit.

All we need is an existing floorplan layout!

70% Reduction in Construction Time

Every wall panel is manufactured off-site with mechanical, electricl, and plumbing integrated into each wall panel. Additionally, each panel has adjustable joints to expand and shrink accordingly. All these features in our wall panel reduces installation time and the manpower required for kit installation.

60% Increase in Productivity

All wall panels are manufactured off-site. This streamlines the construction process thus minimizing delays and ensuring each minute on site is productive.

20% Reduction in Costs

Saving time in design and construction phases automatically saves costs. However, we also source our materials locally which reduces distribution costs. Furthermore, each unit is positioned in such a way that washrooms and kitchens are close to the building's core in order to minimize mechanical, electrical, and plumbing work. Using a bottoms-up approach, we have determined that our prices are 20% cheaper than our competitiors!
